Tuhin Nayak — Founder & Creative Director, Velora Visuals

🏆 Youngest Entrepreneur of Bengal 2026 🏆 The Bengal Excellence Award

For Tuhin Nayak, photography has never been just about capturing images — it has always been about preserving emotions, celebrating stories, and creating timeless visual legacies.

With over four years of experience, Tuhin has built his craft on a rare mix of business acumen and creative vision — pairing a BBA with a B.Sc. in VFX & Film Making. His journey began at J.G. Driven to redefine premium visual storytelling, he founded Velora Visuals on 20 January 2026 — built on creativity, precision, and uncompromising quality. Today, he leads the studio with one simple mission: to create visuals that will be cherished for generations to come.

The Legacy

Chairman Emeritus

Nirmal Kumar Nayak — Chairman Emeritus | Founder, J.G. City Event Management

Behind every enduring institution stands a visionary whose unwavering dedication, integrity, and perseverance shape its legacy. With a career spanning over four decades, Mr. Nirmal Kumar Nayak has earned immense respect as a photographer, journalist, entrepreneur, and mentor across the media and event industries.

His journey began in 1983 as a professional Event Photographer, capturing countless milestones with technical skill and artistic vision. In 1999, he expanded into journalism as a Media Reporter, and today serves as a Senior Crime Reporter — a role reflecting decades of credibility and commitment to ethical reporting.

Driven by entrepreneurial vision, he founded J.G. City Event Management in 2008, growing it into a trusted, multi-specialty brand. Continuing that legacy, Velora Visuals was established in 2026 as its flagship premium unit — carrying forward the same values of innovation, creativity, and perfection into the next generation of cinematic storytelling.

As Chairman, Mr. Nayak continues to provide strategic direction and mentorship to the organization. More than a founder, he is a visionary leader whose enduring commitment to integrity, discipline, and excellence remains the cornerstone of J.G. City Event Management and Velora Visuals.
